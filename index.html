Royal Educational Charitable Trust is one of the fastest growing educational group and student advisory organization,which have 4 branches in Ernakulam District (Kaloor,Tripunithura and Kolenchery),since its inception in 2012. Royal Educational Charitable Trust is registered under the Public Charitable Trust act 1882 with Reg. No: 551/12 to Govt. of Kerala.
Royal Educational Charitable Trust is affiliated to CTDS (Council for Technical Development Continuing Academic Educational & Scientific Studies, Collaborative Training Partner with NIESBUD [Under MINISTRY OF SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP] ). CTDS Courses are conducted on Kaloor,Tripunithura and Kolenchery Branches. All types of Technical and Paramedical Courses are offering on these Branches.These branches provides job oriented courses with in a short duration. We also assures 100% placement with training.
Royal Educational Charitable Trust is also affiliated to C-DIT (Centre for Development of Imaging Technology,Govt. of Kerala)..C-DIT courses are conducted on Tripunithura Branch. This branch provides Software Courses..C-DIT Issues Govt. approved certificates for the students.In Kolenchery Branch,Trust is affiliated to KERALA STATE RUTRONIX.for Conducting all Software,Hardware and Animation Courses.
Trust is a sister concern of Royal Associates and Computer Center and conducts KERALA STATE ROUTRONIX courses in Kaloor ( Software, Hardware and Animation) and Tripunithura (Hardware and Animation Only). The Trust‘s new head office building was inaugrated by Hon:Civil Supplies & Registration Minister Anoop Jacob in the Presence of Coordinators of CTDP,AIMS,Surabhi and RUTRONIX , IGNOU Regional Director & Asst.Director, Councilors, Education Standing Committee Chairman, Charity Workers and Different Party Leaders.

Charity of Trust

On the behalf of Inaugration ,the Trust give free education in computer courses for selected 10 students from each 74 wards of Cochin Corporation and gave Psc,Employment valued certificate of KERALA STATE RUTRONIX .The certificates are distributed with job offer letter to students by the cochin corporation welfare standing committee chairperson Gracy Joseph. Also,Trust gives the pension for old age citizens on every month and giving momentos for the top scorers of Tenth and Plus Two students of Mar Michael Jacobite Syrian Church,Vettithara.The Trust give facilities and support for the handicapped ,financial backward students for their studies and to give better job opportunity. Trust also provide free counseling for students before selecting a course and the councilor will give proper directions for their better future.Many other charity works are doing by the Trust mainly for the poor people.

ROYAL ASSOCIATES & COMPUTER CENTER

Royal Associates & Computer Center was a very small unit focused on providing the Youth unique education in computer at affordable fees from 2000 onwards. In 2004, Royal Associates & Computer Center is affiliated to KERALA STATE RUTRONIX. Royal Associates & Computer Center started with 3 students and now around ten thousands of students completed their courses with high marks and got job through the organization in the field of IT, Fashion Design, Animation, Networking, Hardware etc. Also PSC, Employment approved certificates of KERALA STATE RUTRONIX helps students to get jobs in Government sectors easily.

Royal Associates & Computer Center is an Authorized Training Center (ATC) of KERALA STATE RUTRONIX and have 3 branches in Kaloor, Tripunithura and kolenchery. In Kaloor, Kolenchery branches we have Software, Hardware and Animation courses and in Tripunithura branch we have Hardware and Animation courses only. In Kolenchery, it is affiliated to Royal Educational Charitable trust. We got State and District 1st Prizes around 5 times from KERALA STATE RUTRONIX for “Best Institute in Kerala”.

ROYAL COLLEGE

Royal College is the Sister Concern of ROYAL EDUCATIONAL CHARITABLE TRUST. Royal College Provides SSLC,PLUS TWO,B.A,B.Com,M.Com,Other Courses. Royal College Provides Some of the U.G & P.G Programmes (B.A & B.Com & Others)under Private Registration of Mahatma Gandhi(M.G) University and SSLC, Plus Two under NIOS(National Institute of Open Schooling).We also Provide Regular Plus One & Plus Two under Kerala Board.Students who are aged 14 years can appear for SSLC and also NIOS provides a chance for completing their Plus Two in One Year. After Completing their SSLC & Plus Two they got Central Government approved Certificate of NIOS.

ROYAL EMPLOYMENT SERVICE

Royal Employment Service is a divison of Royal Educational Charitable Trust. It is one of the largest manpower database managers and have an enviable track record of placements, which is offered by brand companies.The Employment Service has a primary objective of providing an interface between the employees and the employers. It sponsers the candidate of required qualification,experience and skill set for various jobs within the shortest time.
The Royal Employment Service strives to achieve its objective through the process of registration,selection and advising of eligible candidates against notified vacancies. Also, it gives counselling and proper direction for students those studies at Royal Educational Charitable Trust and assures 100% placement.
